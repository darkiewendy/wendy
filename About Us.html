WHY MATH

I am a tutor who saw the need in my community for my services. It’s a means for the individuals in my community to gain a better understanding of the subject while learning in a friendly no pressure environment.


WHY THE BUSINESS STARTED.

Wendy-Ann began tutoring her daughter and one of her friends who were having problems in Math. I found out from the kids that there were other kids in the community with the same problems. Soon something that started just to help a child in the community turned into something I was passionate about and led me into wanting to start a tutoring business. Ann’s tutoring Academy will start as a home-based business at #12 Hope Place, San Juan. We offer both online and one on one sessions,these sessions can be individually or in group form.


We offer Math tutoring and our target audience are primary and secondary students and young adults who are struggling, frustrated or just need a little help in Math Hours of operation from 4pm to 7pm, Mondays to Saturdays. Our classes would be available through social platforms such as Zoom and Google Classroom. We can be contacted at our website and email address. At the moment I’m the only service provider of such services in my community. I will offer affordable prices and a well-developed lesson plan for each individual or group. Being a long- standing member of the community, known for helping the kids in the village with their assignment.


HOW ANN’S TUTORING ACADEMY DIFFERS FROM COMPETITION

Ann’s tutoring Academy will be a community based business which offers low prices and convenient payment plans. We will also have specialized lesson plans and good rapport for potential clients. I am a long standing member of the community and I'm knowledgeable in the subject of Mathematics. To summarize, Ann's Tutoring Academy is a reliable institution offering affordable, good services and knowledge in the subject field.
